About Us

Step into the captivating realm of virtual reality gaming! Prepare to be amazed by the remarkable story of Sachin Jaspal, Manoj Tyagi, and Ashish Antil – three bold individuals who defied convention and dropped out of college to embark on an extraordinary adventure. Together, they have harnessed their collective passion and creativity to create a groundbreaking VR gaming console hub.

This remarkable venture epitomizes the extraordinary achievements that arise when audacious dreamers push boundaries and embrace the unconventional. Get ready to embark on an unforgettable journey into the realms of virtual reality with their groundbreaking console hub at the forefront.

Founders

Olfun is the dream of 3 college dropouts, we didn't want to start a life with a 9-5 culture, we wanted that 24hrs job for which we would be working on us and on our dream. We wanted to work together for ourselves, and our enthusiasm for gaming led us to the kingpin business of gaming, the VR world. After successfully acing the VR world, we are now expanding into Arcade, Soft games, simulation, and many other areas.

The hard effort of the last four years has finally paid off, and the result is OLFUN, Fun For All..
