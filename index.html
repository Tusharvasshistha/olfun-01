About OLFun

Welcome to the world of virtual reality gaming! In a tale that combines passion, determination, and a touch of rebellious spirit, three college dropouts, Sachin Jaspal, Manoj Tyagi, and Ashish Antil, have embarked on an extraordinary journey. Together, they have set out to redefine the gaming industry with their groundbreaking VR gaming console hub.


In a world where traditional gaming consoles dominate, these three visionaries dared to challenge the status quo. Fueled by their love for gaming and their desire to create immersive experiences, they embarked on a path less traveled. Unencumbered by the constraints of a formal education, they harnessed their creativity and untapped potential to carve their own destiny.


OLFun promises to be a haven for gamers seeking escapism and thrills. It will offer a vast library of virtual reality games, each meticulously curated to deliver unforgettable experiences. From breathtaking adventures in far-flung galaxies to heart-pounding battles in fantastical worlds, their console hub will be the gateway to a limitless realm of possibilities.

FAQs

Are there any age restrictions for playing VR games at your studio?

There are no age restrictions for playing VR games at our studio. We welcome people of all ages to enjoy the immersive world of virtual reality gaming. Whether you're young or young at heart, come and have a fantastic time exploring virtual reality at our studio!

What is the duration of a typical VR gaming session?

The duration of a typical VR gaming session at our studio varies depending on the game. On average, sessions range from 10 to 25 minutes. Each game offers a unique and immersive experience within this timeframe, ensuring you have an exciting and memorable virtual reality gaming session.

Are there different game options available to choose from?

Absolutely! We offer a wide range of game options for you to choose from at our studio. Whether you're into action-packed adventures, sports simulations, puzzle-solving challenges, or thrilling horror experiences, we have multiple games available to cater to your preferences and ensure an exciting VR gaming session.

Do you offer multiplayer VR gaming experiences?

Yes, we provide multiplayer VR gaming experiences at our studio. You can team up with friends or other players to embark on cooperative adventures, engage in competitive gameplay, or simply enjoy virtual social interactions. Experience the excitement of multiplayer VR gaming and make unforgettable memories together!

Can I save my progress in a game and resume it later?

Absolutely! Our VR gaming system allows you to save your progress in a game, allowing you to resume from where you left off during your next session. You can pick up where you left off, continue your adventure, and strive to achieve your gaming goals without any worries about losing your progress.

Can I rent out the studio for private VR gaming sessions or events?

Yes, you can rent out our studio for private VR gaming sessions or events. Whether you want to host a birthday party, team-building event, or any other special occasion, we offer options to accommodate your needs. Contact us to discuss availability, pricing, and arrangements for your private VR gaming experience.
